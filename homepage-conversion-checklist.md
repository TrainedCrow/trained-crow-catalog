# Homepage Conversion Checklist

Use this quick pass to find visible conversion blockers before sending more traffic to a homepage.

## Above The Fold

- Is the offer clear?
- Is the audience clear?
- Is the next step obvious?
- Is there a reason to trust the page?

## Message Clarity

- Does the page use plain language?
- Does it explain the buyer problem?
- Does it avoid vague claims?
- Does it connect benefits to real use cases?

## CTA Flow

- Is the primary CTA easy to find?
- Is there one main action?
- Does the CTA text describe what happens next?
- Is there enough reassurance near the CTA?

## Trust

- Are delivery, support, scope, or limits clear?
- Are any claims evidence-based?
- Are staged or coming-soon offers clearly marked?
