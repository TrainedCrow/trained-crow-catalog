# Common Landing Page Mistakes

Landing pages often lose buyers through small visible problems rather than one dramatic failure.

## Mistake 1: The Page Does Not Say What It Is Fast Enough

The first screen should make the offer, audience, and next action easy to understand.

## Mistake 2: The CTA Arrives Before Trust

A button is easier to click when the page has already answered why the product is useful, credible, and safe to try.

## Mistake 3: The Page Lists Features Without Buyer Context

Features work better when each one is tied to a clear buyer problem.

## Mistake 4: The Page Hides Proof Or Reassurance

Trust signals, support details, delivery expectations, and simple disclaimers reduce hesitation.

## Mistake 5: Everything Looks Equally Important

If every section competes for attention, the buyer has to do too much sorting.
