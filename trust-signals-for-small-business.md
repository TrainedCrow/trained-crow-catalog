# Trust Signals For Small Business Websites

Trust signals help buyers feel that a page is real, clear, and safe enough to continue.

## Useful Trust Signals

- clear offer wording
- real contact or support information
- transparent delivery expectations
- plain disclaimers
- specific product contents
- clear refund or support boundaries where relevant
- non-exaggerated proof

## What To Avoid

- fake testimonials
- fake reviews
- invented client logos
- unsupported revenue claims
- aggressive scarcity
- vague guarantees

## Above-The-Fold Trust

The first screen should answer:

- What is this?
- Who is it for?
- Why should I trust it?
- What happens if I click?
